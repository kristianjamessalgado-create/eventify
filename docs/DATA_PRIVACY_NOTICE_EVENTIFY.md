# DATA PRIVACY NOTICE

## EVENTIFY - School Events Monitoring System

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effectivity Date:** April 23, 2026

This Data Privacy Notice explains how EVENTIFY collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR), and related issuances of the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

## 1. Personal Data Collected

EVENTIFY may collect the following personal data:

- Account Information: full name, school email, role, department, and generated user code.
- Authentication Information: password hash and login/session metadata.
- Student Profile Information: course/program and year level (for student accounts).
- Event Participation Information: registration/RSVP entries, attendance records, and feedback submitted by users.
- Multimedia Information: event photo uploads, caption text, upload metadata, and publish status.
- System Logs: timestamps, user actions, and security-related technical records.

## 2. Sensitive Personal Information

EVENTIFY does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as government-issued IDs, health records, or biometric data) as defined under Philippine law. Users are advised not to submit sensitive data in free-text fields.

## 3. Purpose of Collection (Legitimate Purpose)

Personal data is collected and processed for legitimate school and system operations, including:

- account registration, verification, and access control;
- event creation, approval, publication, and participation monitoring;
- attendance recording and event feedback collection;
- multimedia documentation and publication of event photos;
- system security, incident response, audit trails, and report generation.

## 4. Data Use and Processing

Personal data is processed only for school event management and operational purposes. Processing includes:

- user authentication and role-based dashboard access;
- event workflow processing (organizer, admin, multimedia, student);
- attendance validation and event history tracking;
- publication controls for event photo galleries;
- logging and monitoring to detect unauthorized or suspicious activity.

Processing is limited to authorized users and system administrators with legitimate access.

## 5. Personal Information Controller (PIC)

The school/project owner operating EVENTIFY serves as the Personal Information Controller (PIC), responsible for determining the purpose and means of processing personal data in the system.

## 6. Data Retention

EVENTIFY retains data only for as long as reasonably necessary for academic, operational, and compliance purposes, subject to school policy.

- Account records are retained while accounts remain active and for audit continuity when necessary.
- Event, attendance, and media records are retained for reporting and documentation requirements.
- Security and activity logs are retained for incident response and accountability.

When retention is no longer necessary, data is securely archived, anonymized, blocked, or disposed according to policy and legal requirements.

## 7. Security Measures

EVENTIFY implements reasonable organizational, technical, and physical safeguards, including:

- encrypted password hashing and secure session handling;
- role-based access controls and approval workflows;
- CSRF protection and server-side input validation;
- activity logging and monitoring for traceability;
- controlled access to student-only or role-restricted routes.

## 8. Data Subject Rights

In accordance with RA 10173, data subjects may exercise the following rights, subject to applicable limitations:

- Right to be informed
- Right to access
- Right to object
- Right to correction/rectification
- Right to erasure or blocking (when legally permissible)
- Right to data portability (when applicable)
- Right to file a complaint before the National Privacy Commission

## 9. Contact for Privacy Concerns

For privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints, users may contact the school/project owner through official school channels (e.g., designated IT office or Data Protection Officer, if assigned).

Users may also consult the National Privacy Commission at: https://privacy.gov.ph/
